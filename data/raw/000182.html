\nMatt Lucas was born on March 5, 1974 in Paddington, London, England as Matthew Richard Lucas. He is an actor and writer, known for Alice in Wonderland (2010), Bridesmaids (2011) and Little Britain (2003). He was previously married to Kevin McGee. \n See full bio »\n \n

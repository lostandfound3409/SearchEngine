Watership Down

\n\n\t\t
\n\t\t\tOctober 31, 2012\t\t\t·\n\t\t\tby novelsummary\t\t\t·\n\t\t\tin Classics.\t\t\t·\n\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t
\n\n\t
\n\t\t

\n

A\xc2\xa0phenomenal worldwide bestseller for over thirty years, Richard Adams’s Watership Down is a timeless classic and one of the most beloved novels of all time. Set in England’s Downs, a once idyllic rural landscape, this stirring tale of adventure, courage and survival follows a band of very special creatures on their flight from the intrusion of man and the certain destruction of their home. Led by a stouthearted pair of brothers, they journey forth from their native Sandleford Warren through the harrowing trials posed by predators and adversaries, to a mysterious promised land and a more perfect society.

\n

To spare a repeat intro, let\xe2\x80\x99s do a little merging here.

\n

After the harrowing events of Brown Bear Brown Bear What Do You See? the remaining wildlife decide they must flee before they too are shot down. \xc2\xa0A family of Rabbits decides to start immediately, and sets off.

\n

The first obstacle is the busy road cutting them off from the rest of their shrinking forest, and the distant mountains. \xc2\xa0The rabbits have noticed that during the nighttime, less metal monsters travel down the hard surface, so that is when they decide to cross. \xc2\xa0While halfway across the stretch of pavement, the two bright lights speed towards the family. \xc2\xa0Most of the rabbits take off, hopping to freedom but the youngest, Wiggle is frozen with fear. \xc2\xa0Mommy Rabbit turns back to save her, Daddy Rabbit not far behind. \xc2\xa0They shove her out of harms way, but are crushed moments later.

\n

The family continues onward, convinced that the mountains will bring them the sanctuary they crave.

\n

This half of the woods only lasts for 27 hops before the rabbits encounter the newly flattened field, inhabited by giant, fat deer-like creatures. \xc2\xa0These black and white spotted giants refuse the rabbit’s petition to cross their field, so they begin to circle the fence line. \xc2\xa0They are discovered by the farmer’s dog, and barely outrun the beast.

\n

More fields are ahead, and are crossed. \xc2\xa0The rabbits discover that while the cows always refuse to allow passage through their field, they don’t do much when the family crosses anyways. \xc2\xa0The rabbits have almost reached the mountain.

\n

One morning a hawk swoops down and carries Wiggle away.

\n

Another morning humans with their deathly shooting sticks follow them for miles, with more dogs chasing the bedraggled family. \xc2\xa0A cousin trips, and is killed.

\n

Somehow, the remainders reach the mountain. \xc2\xa0They find other rabbit families, who show them to an empty meadow, perfect for digging a den. \xc2\xa0The family settles down, thankful that their ordeal has finally ended.

\n

Michael Jackson tribute show delayed after star\xe2\x80\x99s operation

\n

11:06 22 February 2015

\n

\n By Alan Davies\n

\n
\n \n\t\t\t\t\n \n
\n

Jackson Live in Concert at the Gordon Craig Theatre has had be to rescheduled after Ben - the show\'s Michael Jackson - had an operation

\n

Archant

\n
\n
\n \t\t \t

A touring tribute show to the music of Michael Jackson scheduled to rock two Hertfordshire venues has been postponed until later in the year.

Jackson Live in Concert

Jackson Live in Concert was set to take the stage at Watford Colosseum on Thursday, and then Stevenage\xe2\x80\x99s Gordon Craig Theatre on Friday, March 13.

But the show\xe2\x80\x99s Michael Jackson was taken ill during the opening performance of the 2015 tour and needed surgery.

A statement from the show\xe2\x80\x99s promoters said: \xe2\x80\x9cUnfortunately Ben, the star of the show, became unwell after his opening performance for the 2015 tour and had to have an operation.

\xe2\x80\x9cHe is very keen to get his sequinned glove and black fedora hat on as soon as he can and we all wish him a speedy recovery.\xe2\x80\x9d

Jackson Live in Concert at the Gordon Craig Theatre has had be to rescheduled after Ben - the show\'s Michael Jackson - has had an operation

The Watford date has been rearranged for Sunday, May 24, and the Stevenage show will now take place on Sunday, June 21.

Organisers added: \xe2\x80\x9cBen and the team are very sorry for any inconvenience caused and are looking forward to seeing everyone later in the year.\xe2\x80\x9d

Tickets for the Stevenage show cost \xc2\xa321.

Tickets for the Watford Colosseum date are \xc2\xa319.50 and \xc2\xa317.50.

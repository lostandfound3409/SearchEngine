The members of Led Zeppelin may not have any interest in playing together these days, but the 7- to 12-year-olds of the Louisville Leopard Percussionists are ready to take on the Zeppelin catalog. The percussion ensemble, consisting of about 55 kids from the Louisville, Ky., area, recently posted an impressive video of the group rehearsing three Zeppelin classics:\xc2\xa0Kashmir,\xc2\xa0Ocean and\xc2\xa0Immigrant Song.

\n

\n

Zeppelin guitarist Jimmy Page even posted the video to his Facebook page over the weekend, saying it was “too good not to share.”

\n

\n

Last year, the group posted a steel-drum medley of We the Kings’\xc2\xa0Check Yes Juliet and Pharrell Williams’\xc2\xa0Happy.

\n World Stunt Awards\n 2011\n

\n \n \n \n \n \n
\n Nominated
\n Taurus Award\n 		\n Best Overall Stunt by a Stunt Woman
\n Tarah Paige\n
\n
\n

\nAlice falls through the rabbit hole. In the first part of the stunt, a stuntwoman falls 20\' through... More\n

\n

\nAlice falls through the rabbit hole. In the first part of the stunt, a stuntwoman falls 20\' through a 2 \xc2\xbd\' opening to a porta pit. In the second part of the stunt, a she is on wires, falls 25\' to an Aussie trampoline and bounces to a porta pit. In the third part of the stunt, a stuntwoman jumps 20\' thru a breakaway ceiling onto porta pits. Then, the stuntwoman was hung 12\' on a quick release, cut away to a curved set piece and hit a floor with no padding. CGI was used throughout the sequence of falls and landings.

The Catcher in the rye

\t\t\t\t\t\t

11 Apr 2013 1 Comment

\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t\tby sentientbeingblogger\n\t\t\t\t\tin Critiques\n\t\t\t\t\tTags: book, Holden, j d salinger, literature, Salinger, The Catcher in the rye\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t


\n

\n

So\xc2\xa0 in my last post I mentioned about embracing the \xe2\x80\x9cnoise in my head\xe2\x80\x9d & guess what ? I kinda lucked out recently regarding that!! I\xe2\x80\x99ll tell you how!

\n

Ok so my MBA is all done & I am back home, vacationing for a month before I join the big bad corporate world .I decided to start reading \xe2\x80\x9cThe catcher in the Rye\xe2\x80\x9d by J D Salinger\xc2\xa0 & OMG!!\xe2\x80\x93

\n

Before you read further, a caveat!- I am soo not revealing the plot. That\xe2\x80\x99s the worst thing you can do for the books’s potential readers! Here is my two cents on my takeaway from it. If you find the general direction interesting, you should pick up your own copy or else wiki it for the plot will you ;) or better still if you have read the book already, figure if someone else felt the same way too.

\n

\xc2\xa0So ok back to OMG!! – If ever someone had a constant voice in their head.. this unending monologue with themselves.. Amusing , depressing , enlightening , omnipresent\xc2\xa0 , it was The Protagonist in this book, this dude named\xc2\xa0 Holden Caulfield!!

\n

His conscious, lets call it conscious for fun can we? His conscious is fun! Depressing, but so very like our own voice in the head. I fell in love with it. Headsup though \xe2\x80\x93 this fellow finds just about everything & nearly everyone quite depressing & phony (He really loves this word). But his thought process & his flashpan opinions are so captivating!!

\n

I read through about 40 pages waiting for the story to start, not complaining, cause I was enjoying \xe2\x80\x9cthe noise in this dude\xe2\x80\x99s head\xe2\x80\x9d cause you know I thought I\xe2\x80\x99ll understand him better.. Was all Context building I thought, till I realized hell !! It seems this is it!! This perpetual dialogue he is having with himself is the story! His life, his observations on the small, the mundane, his loneliness, and his continuous rant is the story!!

\n

& you know why I kept reading? Cause I love the rant! I know the rant! I know we kinda suppress the rant, what with all of us conditioned to be \xe2\x80\x9cwith it\xe2\x80\x9d & being all hunky dory positive all the time. In the words of the dude in the book \xe2\x80\x93 It goddamn makes me wanna puke!! Imagine that! Although I use \xe2\x80\x9crant\xe2\x80\x9d to describe his soliloquy , I don\xe2\x80\x99t mean to trivialize it in any way. Thing is – The subject of his outpourings change abruptly, making Holden sound very close to be just a regular whiner, but if you read with an empathetic\xc2\xa0 approach , You\xe2\x80\x99ll kinda find yourself in those sentences somewhere. We all have experienced utter loneliness & confusion some time or other.

\n

\xc2\xa0 I think the secret to enjoying this book , is to kinda say , Oh Hol Ol boy! I see what u are talking about . I hate em buggers too! Which one? Oh all of em , no one in particular. Let\xe2\x80\x99s talk . No really!! ;)

\n

In fact there is this passage in the book , where Holden talks about why he likes his fave authors & the criteria is \xe2\x80\x93 well when u put down the book , you kinda want to call the author like he is an ol buddy & have a regular friendly conversation! I think it made Holden a bit more\xc2\xa0likable\xc2\xa0just for that! No idea why!

\n

Honestly though, I thought I would be depressed, cause of his book. Weirdly enough it was liberating!! Maybe it\xe2\x80\x99s a little validation for talking to myself\xc2\xa0 & shooting weird looks to people every now & then (Sometimes its cause I don\xe2\x80\x99t have my glasses on , but that\xe2\x80\x99s something else altogether)\xc2\xa0 ;)

\n

Literary critics will have differing opinions & maybe Old Salinger (if he ever came to know my verdict on his work) would be all like \xe2\x80\x93 Hell no!! That\xe2\x80\x99s not the idea! But I\xe2\x80\x99m just saying! Dude I loved your 200 page crazyass rant .

\n

I would love to know what you guys thought about the book! \xc2\xa0Isn’t\xc2\xa0that\xc2\xa0 a wonderful\xc2\xa0 thing about a book? Different people take away different ideas from the same book \xc2\xa0:)

\n

or If you\xc2\xa0haven’t read it yet , do you intend to now?

James Bobin’s ALICE IN WONDERLAND: THROUGH THE LOOKING GLASS Begins Production

\n\n

\n by Matt Goldberg    \n August 4, 2014\n

\n
\n\n
\n\n
\n\n \n
\n\n
\n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n \n

Tim Burton‘s Alice in Wonderland is one of the director’s weakest efforts, but it’s also by far his highest grossing.\xc2\xa0 The movie made over $1 billion worldwide at the box office, and I’m surprised it’s taken so long for a sequel to start moving into production.\xc2\xa0 But four years later, production has begun on\xc2\xa0Alice in Wonderland: Through the Looking Glass with James Bobin (The Muppets) in the director’s chair and the original actors returning along with new additions to both the live-action and the voice cast.\xc2\xa0 Plot details are scarce, but the story will “revisit Lewis Carroll\xe2\x80\x99s beloved stories with an all-new new tale that travels back to Underland\xe2\x80\x94and back in Time.”

\n

Hit the jump for the press release, which includes the full cast and who they’re playing as well as the production’s key contributors behind the camera.\xc2\xa0 Alice in Wonderland: Through the Looking Glass opens May 27, 2016.

\n

Here’s the press release:

\n
\n

DISNEY\xe2\x80\x99S \xe2\x80\x9cALICE IN WONDERLAND: THROUGH THE LOOKING GLASS\xe2\x80\x9d KICKS OFF PRODUCTION WITH AWARD-WINNING CAST & PRODUCTION TEAM\xc2\xa0

\n

Filmmakers Return to Beloved Stories of Lewis Carroll for

\n

All-New Adventure to Underland and Beyond\xc2\xa0

\n

BURBANK, Calif. (Aug. 4, 2014) \xe2\x80\x93 Disney\xe2\x80\x99s \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland: Through the Looking Glass\xe2\x80\x9d begins principal photography this week, featuring an award-winning production team and all-star ensemble cast, including Johnny Depp, Anne Hathaway, Mia Wasikowska, Helena Bonham Carter and Sacha Baron Cohen. James Bobin (\xe2\x80\x9cThe Muppets,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cMuppets Most Wanted\xe2\x80\x9d) helms the film, which revisits Lewis Carroll\xe2\x80\x99s beloved stories with an all-new new tale that travels back to Underland\xe2\x80\x94and back in Time. \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland: Through the Looking Glass\xe2\x80\x9d is produced by Joe Roth (\xe2\x80\x9cMaleficent,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cAlice in Wonderland\xe2\x80\x9d), Jennifer Todd (\xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cMemento\xe2\x80\x9d), Suzanne Todd (\xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cMemento\xe2\x80\x9d) and Tim Burton (\xe2\x80\x9cAlice in Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cFrankenweenie\xe2\x80\x9d). John G. Scotti (\xe2\x80\x9cMuppets Most Wanted,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cThe Muppets\xe2\x80\x9d) is the executive producer; Linda Woolverton (\xe2\x80\x9cMaleficent,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cBeauty and the Beast\xe2\x80\x9d) penned the screenplay. Shooting on location and at Shepperton Studios in England, the film is slated for release on May 27, 2016.

\n

The all-star ensemble cast includes:

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Johnny Depp (\xe2\x80\x9cPirates of the Caribbean\xe2\x80\x9d films, \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cMortdecai,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cBlack Mass\xe2\x80\x9d) returns to the big screen as the Mad Hatter\xe2\x80\x94Hatter Tarrant Hightopp.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Anne Hathaway (\xe2\x80\x9cLes Mis\xc3\xa9rables,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cThe Devil Wears Prada,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cRachel\xe2\x80\x99s Getting Married\xe2\x80\x9d) portrays the White Queen\xe2\x80\x94Mirana.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Mia Wasikowska (\xe2\x80\x9cTracks,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cJane Eyre\xe2\x80\x9d and \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland\xe2\x80\x9d) once again appears as Alice Kingsleigh, who\xe2\x80\x99s on a quest to help the Mad Hatter.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Helena Bonham Carter (\xe2\x80\x9cCinderella,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cLes Mis\xc3\xa9rables,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland\xe2\x80\x9d) returns as the Red Queen\xe2\x80\x94Iracebeth.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Rhys Ifans (\xe2\x80\x9cHarry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part I,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cNanny McPhee Returns,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cThe Amazing Spider-Man\xe2\x80\x9d) fills the shoes of Zanik Hightopp, the Mad Hatter\xe2\x80\x99s father.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Matt Lucas (\xe2\x80\x9cThe Labrynth,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland\xe2\x80\x9d) returns to the revelry in a dual role as Tweedledee and Tweedledum.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Ed Speleers (TV\xe2\x80\x99s \xe2\x80\x9cDownton Abbey,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cEragon\xe2\x80\x9d) portrays James Harcourt in the film.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 And Sacha Baron Cohen (\xe2\x80\x9cHugo,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cLes Mis\xc3\xa9rables\xe2\x80\x9d) was called on to bring Time to the big screen.

\n

\xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland: Through the Looking Glass\xe2\x80\x9d also boasts an extraordinary voice cast.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Stephen Fry (\xe2\x80\x9c24: Live Another Day,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cThe Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cSherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland\xe2\x80\x9d) is back as the voice of the Cheshire Cat.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Toby Jones (\xe2\x80\x9cThe Hunger Games: Catching Fire,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cInfamous,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cThe Girl\xe2\x80\x9d) provides the voice of Wilkins.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Alan Rickman (\xe2\x80\x9cHarry Potter\xe2\x80\x9d film series, \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d director/actor/co-writer \xe2\x80\x9cA Little Chaos\xe2\x80\x9d) returns as the voice of the Blue Caterpillar.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Michael Sheen (\xe2\x80\x9cThe Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn\xe2\x80\x9d Parts 1 & 2, \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cThe Queen,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cFrost Nixon\xe2\x80\x9d) once again serves as the voice of the White Rabbit.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Timothy Spall (\xe2\x80\x9cMr. Turner,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cThe Kings Speech,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cHarry Potter,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland\xe2\x80\x9d) returns to voice Bayard.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 BAFTA-winning actor, writer and comedian Paul Whitehouse (BBC\xe2\x80\x99s \xe2\x80\x9cHarry and Paul\xe2\x80\x99s Story of the 2s\xe2\x80\x9d and \xe2\x80\x9cThe Fast Show\xe2\x80\x9d; \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cCorpse Bride\xe2\x80\x9d) is back as the March Hare.

\n

\xc2\xb7\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Barbara Windsor (\xe2\x80\x9cCarry On,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cEastEnders,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland\xe2\x80\x9d) provides the voice of Dormouse again.

\n

The film welcomes back several key members of the \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland\xe2\x80\x9d team, including award-winning composer Danny Elfman (\xe2\x80\x9cFifty Shades of Grey,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cBig Eyes,\xe2\x80\x9d Silver Linings Playbook\xe2\x80\x9d), three-time Oscar\xc2\xae-winning costume designer Colleen Atwood (\xe2\x80\x9cMemoirs of a Geisha,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cChicago\xe2\x80\x9d), who won Oscar, BAFTA and Satellite awards, among others, for her work on \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland,\xe2\x80\x9d and five-time Oscar-winning VFX supervisor Ken Ralston (\xe2\x80\x9cForrest Gump,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cStar Wars Episode VI: Return of the Jedi\xe2\x80\x9d), who won a Satellite Award for best visual effects for \xe2\x80\x9cAlice In Wonderland.\xe2\x80\x9d

\n

Bobin\xe2\x80\x99s award-winning production team also includes Oscar\xc2\xae-winning production designer Dan Hennah (\xe2\x80\x9cKing Kong,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cThe Hobbit\xe2\x80\x9d trilogy, \xe2\x80\x9cThe Lord of the Rings\xe2\x80\x9d trilogy, Oscar for \xe2\x80\x9cThe Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King\xe2\x80\x9d), and Stuart Dryburgh (\xe2\x80\x9cThe Secret Life of Walter Mitty,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cBridget Jones\xe2\x80\x99 Diary,\xe2\x80\x9d Oscar nomination for \xe2\x80\x9cThe Piano\xe2\x80\x9d) as director of photography. The film welcomes Oscar-winning make-up and hair designer Peter King (\xe2\x80\x9cLord of the Rings: Return of the King,\xe2\x80\x9d Oscar nominee for \xe2\x80\x9cThe Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey\xe2\x80\x9d), and Oscar winner Neil Corbould (\xe2\x80\x9cGravity,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cGladiator\xe2\x80\x9d) is special effects supervisor. Andrew Weisblum (\xe2\x80\x9cNoah,\xe2\x80\x9d Oscar nominee \xe2\x80\x9cBlack Swan\xe2\x80\x9d) is editor.

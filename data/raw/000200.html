Goofs

\nWhen Mufasa and Simba are talking at the top of Pride Rock, facing the sun, their shadows stream out behind them, which is correct. However, as Simba says "Everything the light touches", his shadow is in front of him, which is wrong. It should be cast on his right. Also, in the next shot, when Simba and Mufasa are facing south towards the \'shadowy place\', their right sides are in shadow, with the light shining from the left. But it is sunrise, the sun, and therefore the light, should be on their right. See more »\n

The best Disney animated film ever...This film had it all, it was funny, emotional, had family drama, and above all, great animation and songs! My personal favorite character is Rafiki, the Baboon! I still cant forget the line he says to Simbe, "Past can hurt, but as I see it, you can either run from it, or learn from it!" it is so true! i loved Zazu\'s weirdness, and shenzi\'s humor, above all, i loved Pumba\'s innocence and Timon\'s intelligence!

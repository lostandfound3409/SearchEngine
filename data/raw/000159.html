TOY STORY 3 (In Disney Digital 3D\xe2\x84\xa2)

\n

Genre: Animation/Comedy/Adventure

\n

Release Date: June 18, 2010

\n

Voice Talent:\xc2\xa0 Tom Hanks, Tim Allen, Joan Cusack, Don Rickles, Wallace Shawn, John Ratzenberger, Estelle Harris, John Morris, Laurie Metcalf, R. Lee Ermey, Jodi Benson, Ned Beatty, Bonnie Hunt, Timothy Dalton, Jeff Garlin, Whoopi Goldberg and Michael Keaton

\n

Director:\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Lee Unkrich

\n

Producer:\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Darla K. Anderson

\n

Writer:\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 credit TBD

\n

Composer:\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 \xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0\xc2\xa0 Randy Newman

\n

The creators of the beloved \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story\xe2\x80\x9d films re-open the toy box and bring moviegoers back to the delightful world of Woody, Buzz and our favorite gang of toy characters in TOY STORY 3. Woody and Buzz had accepted that their owner Andy would grow up someday, but what happens when that day arrives? In the third installment, Andy is preparing to depart for college, leaving his loyal toys troubled about their uncertain future. Lee Unkrich (co-director of \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story 2\xe2\x80\x9d and \xe2\x80\x9cFinding Nemo\xe2\x80\x9d) directs this highly anticipated film, and Michael Arndt, the Academy Award\xc2\xae-winning screenwriter of \xe2\x80\x9cLittle Miss Sunshine,\xe2\x80\x9d brings his unique talents and comedic sensibilities to the proceedings. Toy Story 3 will be presented in Disney Digital 3D\xe2\x84\xa2 in select theaters.

\n

Notes:

\n

TOY STORY 3 follows the return of Disney\xe2\x97\x8fPixar\xe2\x80\x99s \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story\xe2\x80\x9d and \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story 2\xe2\x80\x9d to the big screen Oct. 2, 2009\xe2\x80\x94this time as a Disney Digital 3D\xc3\xa4 double-feature.

\n

Director Lee Unkrich began at Pixar Animation Studios in 1994 as a film editor on \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story\xe2\x80\x9d and continued on to \xe2\x80\x9cA Bug\xe2\x80\x99s Life\xe2\x80\x9d as the supervising film editor. He made his directing debut in 1999 as co-director of the Golden Globe\xc2\xae-winning \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story 2.\xe2\x80\x9d\xc2\xa0 Unkrich co-directed \xe2\x80\x9cMonsters, Inc.\xe2\x80\x9d and served as co-director and supervising film editor of the Oscar\xc2\xae-winning animated feature \xe2\x80\x9cFinding Nemo.\xe2\x80\x9d

\n

The original \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story\xe2\x80\x9d voice cast is set to return in TOY STORY 3, including John Ratzenberger (voice of Hamm), who is the only actor to voice a character in all 11 Disney\xe2\x80\xa2Pixar films. Also returning is Oscar\xc2\xae-winning songwriter and composer Randy Newman (\xe2\x80\x9cToy Story,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story 2,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9cMonsters, Inc.\xe2\x80\x9d).

\n

\xe2\x80\x9cToy Story,\xe2\x80\x9d originally released on Nov. 22, 1995, was the first fully computer animated feature film and the highest grossing movie of the year with nearly $192 million domestically and $362 million worldwide. It was nominated for three Oscars\xc2\xae and two Golden Globes\xc2\xae.

\n

\xe2\x80\x9cToy Story\xe2\x80\x9d director John Lasseter was awarded a Special Achievement Award (Oscar\xc2\xae) by the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences for his \xe2\x80\x9cinspired leadership of the Pixar \xe2\x80\x98Toy Story\xe2\x80\x99 team, resulting in the first feature-length computer-animated film.”

\n

\xe2\x80\x9cToy Story 2\xe2\x80\x9d is the first film ever to be entirely created, mastered and exhibited digitally. It was also the first animated sequel to gross more than its original, breaking opening weekend box office records in the U.S., UK and Japan, becoming the highest grossing animated release of 1999 with more than $245 million in domestic box office receipts and $485 million worldwide.\xc2\xa0 It was nominated for an Academy Award\xc2\xae and two Golden Globes\xc2\xae, winning the Golden Globe for Best Motion Picture/Comedy Musical. It won a Grammy\xc2\xae for Best Song Written for a Motion Picture, Television or other Visual Media (Randy Newman, \xe2\x80\x9cWhen She Loved Me\xe2\x80\x9d).

\n

The creators of the beloved \xe2\x80\x9cToy Story\xe2\x80\x9d films re-open the toy box with TOY STORY 3, a comedic and heartwarming adventure in Disney Digital 3D\xe2\x84\xa2, bringing moviegoers back to the world of Woody, Buzz and our favorite gang of toy characters as Andy prepares to leave for college.

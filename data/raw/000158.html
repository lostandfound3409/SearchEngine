New Disney Pixar Toy Story 3 Movie Poster Released

\r\n
April 28, 2010 • By Disney Dreaming
\r\n
\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n
\r\n
\r\n
\n


\n

Disney/Pixar has released a new Toy Story 3 movie poster including a larger number of the characters than on many of the other posters released so far. The movie is set to hit theaters on June 18, 2010 – so we’re getting closer!!!

\r\n\r\n\r\n
\r\n\n

    Marvel\'s Five-Year Plan For The Avengers To Rescue The Movies

    \n\n\n\n\n\t\t\t\n\n\t\t\t
    \n\t

    In order to understand Marvel\xe2\x80\x99s approach in promoting the highly anticipated The Avengers movie, it\xe2\x80\x99s necessary to step back in time to 2008.

    \n

    Five years ago, Marvel executives developed a comprehensive plan involving four superheroes to culminate with one \xe2\x80\x9cuber\xe2\x80\x9d movie, says Marvel\xe2\x80\x99s Paul Gitter. \xe2\x80\x9cWe took the superheroes \xe2\x80\x94 Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, and Hulk \xe2\x80\x94 that we felt were the most relatable, relevant, and aspirational to build into one package. Every Marvel movie since 2008 was created with the full intention of this super franchise.\xe2\x80\x9d

    \n

    Yet, promoting a movie with four spotlight-hogging superheroes is not without its challenges. \xe2\x80\x9cIt\xe2\x80\x99s definitely a complex brand,\xe2\x80\x9d says Marvel\xe2\x80\x99s Paul Gitter. \xe2\x80\x9cInstead of just one, you have four ingredients [Iron Man, Hulk, Captain America, Thor] to brand merchandise and get SKUs [at retail].\xe2\x80\x9d

    \n

    As a result, The Avengers emphasizes the \xe2\x80\x9cCore Four\xe2\x80\x9d rather than any one specific superhero in TV and print advertisements. And there\xe2\x80\x99s a concentrated focus to showcase all members equally on Avengers merchandise.

    \n

    Marvel is also releasing individual character merchandise in \xe2\x80\x9cwaves\xe2\x80\x9d to help ensure superheroes receive their turn in the spotlight. For example, the first wave of The Avengers toys by Hasbro (which holds the toy license for the property) is heavy on Iron Man and Captain America. The second wave will see an increase in toys for Thor and the villain Loki, while the third wave will emphasize the least known characters, Hawkeye and Black Widow, says Hasbro\xe2\x80\x99s Daniel Benkwitt. Retailers will also receive \xe2\x80\x9csurprise\xe2\x80\x9d boxes of Avengers toys in which the character SKUs are just randomly thrown together.

    \n

    Nonetheless, fans inherently prefer certain superheroes over others. \xe2\x80\x9cIt\xe2\x80\x99s just like you would think \xe2\x80\x94 Iron Man and Captain America sell more than Thor,\xe2\x80\x9d says Gitter. However, The Hulk may soon surpass them all. Marvel executives have been \xe2\x80\x9cpleasantly surprised by the phenomenal response\xe2\x80\x9d to this green superhero, and expect this fan affinity to further strengthen once the movie opens. \xe2\x80\x9cHis sales are up in a major way. We repositioned him from where he was always misunderstood to now depicting him in a more heroic and aspirational manner,\xe2\x80\x9d says Gitter.

    \n

    If Hulk\xe2\x80\x99s successful turnaround continues, Gitter says Marvel \xe2\x80\x9cwill spin him off to a stand-alone program next year,\xe2\x80\x9d presumably supported by a big budget franchise movie in 2015. The entertainment studio is also exploring ways to promote Hulk as a \xe2\x80\x9ccorporate icon,\xe2\x80\x9d similar to MetLife\xe2\x80\x99s usage of Peanuts\xe2\x80\x99 Snoopy, where The Hulk\xe2\x80\x99s image conveys a message about channeling strength to overcome workplace challenges.

    \n

    Marvel\xe2\x80\x99s multi-year strategy also played a role in amassing more than $100 million in promotional support for the Avengers film. Starting in 2008, Marvel approached partners with the understanding that relationships would be multi-year, multi-movie agreements. \xe2\x80\x9cThere are many opportunities for brands to align with the big commercial in the sky [Gitter\xe2\x80\x99s term for blockbuster movies], but we want to develop sustainable relationships [with our promotional partners].\xe2\x80\x9d As such, Harley-Davidson, Dr Pepper, and Acura are a few of the brands who initially supported individual Marvel franchises and are back again for The Avengers.

    \n

    This hefty promotional support meant Marvel had to be proactive to avoid superhero oversaturation. \xe2\x80\x9cWe looked at every retailer strategically to see how can we be different. We think we came up with a nice mix,\xe2\x80\x9d says Gitter.

    \n

    Walmart\xe2\x80\x99s program, for instance, centers on technology and the Marvel Avengers Super Hero Augmented Reality mobile app. Shoppers have instant access to Hawkeye, but can only \xe2\x80\x9cunlock\xe2\x80\x9d Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, the Hulk, and Black Widow in the aisles of the store. Comparatively, Marvel worked with clothing retailer Aeropostale to develop visually distinctive displays to catch the attention of \xe2\x80\x9chip and trendy\xe2\x80\x9d teens. Target\xe2\x80\x99s The Avengers initiative utilizes broadcast and traditional media, and Toys R Us is about the in-store experience, and has opened Avengers-themed in-store boutiques at 600 locations.

    \n

    While The Avengers\xe2\x80\x99 May 4 opening marks the end of the current multi-year plan, Marvel has already developed a detailed marketing strategy for the next five years. Whereas this approach saw the feature film as the finish line, the upcoming campaign will \xe2\x80\x9csynergize\xe2\x80\x9d the Avengers into a \xe2\x80\x9cmulti-platform brand that can live 365 days a year.\xe2\x80\x9d To that end, Marvel is introducing digital initiatives, new print and comic book titles, and The Avengers animated TV series on the  in 2013. Iron Man 3 hits theaters next year, and Captain America and Thor sequels are in the works. Although the Black Widow isn\xe2\x80\x99t yet scheduled for her own stand-alone movie, the character\xe2\x80\x99s lifestyle will inspire a collection of athletic gear and beauty products.

    \n

    It\xe2\x80\x99s also likely that Marvel superheroes will soon be spotted in the theme parks of its corporate parent Disney, and in Broadway shows, similar to Marvel\xe2\x80\x99s Spider-Man: Turn Off The Dark. Gitter doesn\xe2\x80\x99t directly confirm these initiatives, only stating that Marvel is \xe2\x80\x9cexploring all land-based opportunities, including stand-alone shows and attractions.\xe2\x80\x9d

The tracklist alone brings back a flood of memories.  Every song defines certain moments of a very happy time in my life.  It was ever-present for me that autumn.  No matter what memory comes to mind, I can hear this album playing in the background.  I hope we can once again experience another hit like "Music".  It was everywhere.  

\n

 

\n

This album had it all... #1 single, insanely amazing videos, killer dance mixes... and I just adored the cowgirl theme.  It was perfect and I remember being so amazed by all the girls I saw wearing cowboy hats because of her.  Always a trendsetter.  

\n

 

\n

\n

 

\n

\n

 

\n

 

\n

 

\n

Just Googling the images for this post has been a wonderful trip down memory lane.  

\n

 

\n

I remember the Warner Bros site having such an impressively interactive page for this album, where they had the instrumentals of title track broken into individual components, and you could isolate them to make your own remix.  I played with that for hours.  I wish I could see it again...

\n

 

\n

Anyway, appreciate this masterpiece and era!  14 years already!!!

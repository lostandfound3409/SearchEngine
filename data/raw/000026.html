    Lord of the Rings by J. R. R. Tolkien

    \n


    The story of Tolkien\'s powerful ring might be the most popular fantasy book series in English literature.\xc2\xa0Lord of the Rings is the second best-selling novel ever written. The series was incredibly influential on the formation of the fantasy genre in the 1960s and 70s, and as a countercultural mantra in popular culture.\xc2\xa0Like Martin\'s world of Westeros, this is a story that requires dutiful study of the map at the front of the book.

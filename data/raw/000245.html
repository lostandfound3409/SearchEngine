\nINCEPTION is the most rare of movie miracles - an art film that succeeds as blockbuster entertainment. This achievement is born from the gifts of writer/director Christopher Nolan, who displays such a love for filmmaking that audiences cannot help but be enveloped by his rapture. Leonardo DiCaprio proves once again to be America\'s leading man, guiding us through a mind-bending movie-within-a-movie, within-a-movie, within-a-movie - and cinematographer Wally Pfister\'s dazzling images create an indelible dream that demands to be relived again and again.

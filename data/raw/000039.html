And Then There Were None Quotes

\n\t\t\t\t\t

by Saba Seperteladze · January 7, 2015

\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t\t\t\n\n\n\n\t
\n\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t

Extraordinary and gripping novel!
\nIt’s a common way of choosing a writer, to read their most known books. So, of course, I chose And Then There Were None. Agatha truly deserves to be praised, because of her book being in the best-selling mystery novel of all time with 100 million of copies, which is a HUGE achievement.
\nThe book was a remarkable read. It’s an unbelievable feeling when you feel as trapped as those people on that island. Truly a great atmosphere.
\nI’m not trying to be a show off, but at some point I figured it out who was the murderer, but have no worries, I won’t reveal it. I’m also aware of people saying: “Sure Sherlock! I believe you!”. However, I indeed discovered it. To let you know I’ve read and watched numerous amount of mystery stuff, consequently, This is not my first story and I have some knowledge in this genre. Well, that doesn’t turn me into a detective, but, to say it frankly, I guessed it only because I focused on the “why” and not the “how” what helped me a lot. All in All, believe it or not, I somehow managed it!
\nAnyway, I think the fun of reading this kind of books is trying to figure out the the mystery and analyze it flawlessly. Though, it doesn’t matter if you guess right or not, explanation is always rewarding, but still, being correct is more delicious.
\nCertainly, this novel isn’t only a marvelous mystery book but also a scary horror story. Agatha Christie is the queen of mystery, with her fantastic twisted character developing.

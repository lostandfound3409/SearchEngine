Free Family Dinner & Movie | SF

Every third Friday, the OMI Family Resource Center in San Francisco invites you to their free monthly dinner and movie night.

\n

Free, but an\xc2\xa0RSVP is required by calling\xc2\xa0Priscilla at 415.406.1370.\xc2\xa0Space is limited.

\n

Dinner is free, but limited to the first 50 people. The menu varies monthly. Movies starts at 5 pm sharp.

\n

Please note that this is a special monthly event for San Francisco families in need of a night out, and not a free-for-all.\xc2\xa0

\n

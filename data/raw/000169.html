What is the Tomatometer™?

\n
\n
\n

\n The Tomatometer rating – based on the published opinions of hundreds of film and\n television critics – is a trusted measurement of movie and TV programming quality\n for millions of moviegoers. It represents the percentage of professional critic reviews\n that are positive for a given film or television show.\n
